AgroGenius Harvest
Role:
Team Leader — Handled the entire project lifecycle, including SRS documentation and full website development.
Overview:
AgroGenius Harvest is a web platform allowing farmers to purchase farming tools and fertilizers at low cost directly from manufacturers. The idea was to involve local shopkeepers and government schemes for enhanced accessibility.
Features:
  • Buy tools/fertilizers at discounted prices
  • Local delivery and shopkeeper support
  • Manufacturer-to-customer direct model
  • Fully responsive frontend
